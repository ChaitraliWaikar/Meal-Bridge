Food Sharing Network

Volunteer Opportunities

Join our network of volunteers helping to redistribute surplus food to those who need it most. Choose from various time slots and locations across Mumbai.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Make a Real Impact

Every 2-hour shift helps provide approximately 20-30 meals to people in need.

Flexible Schedule

Choose from multiple 2-hour time slots throughout the day that fit your schedule.

No Experience Needed

We provide all necessary training. Just bring your enthusiasm and willingness to help!
