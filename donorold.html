Frequently Asked Questions

What types of food can I donate? +
We accept non-perishable foods like canned goods, pasta, rice, and cereal, as well as fresh produce, dairy, and frozen items in good condition. All items must be unexpired and in their original packaging.
How does pickup scheduling work? +
After submitting your donation form, you'll receive a confirmation with your scheduled pickup date and time. Our volunteers will arrive during the selected time slot. You'll receive reminder notifications via email or SMS.
Can I track where my donation goes? +
Yes! Once your donation is processed, you'll receive updates about which food bank or shelter received your items. We also provide impact statistics showing how many people your donation helped feed.
Do I get a tax receipt for my donation? +
Yes, we provide tax receipts for all documented food donations. The receipt will be emailed to you after your donation has been processed.
How can I volunteer to help with food distribution? +
We're always looking for volunteers! Click on the "Volunteer" link in our footer to see current opportunities and sign up for shifts that fit your schedule.
